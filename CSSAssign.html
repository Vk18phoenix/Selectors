Making Coffee:

  1. Boil milk in pot
  1. Add coffee powder and sugar
  1. Pour the hot coffee into your mug. Add the desired amount of cream.
  2. Gently stir to combine the coffee and cream.

Prawns Biryani:

  1. Marinate cleaned prawns with ginger-garlic paste, spices, and lemon juice.
  1. Parboil basmati rice to retain its texture.
  1. Prepare a flavorful biryani masala base with onions, tomatoes, and essential spices.
  2. Add marinated prawns to the masala base and cook until done.
  3. Layer the cooked rice and prawn mixture in a biryani pot.
  4. Seal and cook on low heat to infuse flavors.
  5. Garnish with fried onions, cilantro, and mint before serving hot.

BBQ Pizza:

  1. Roll out pizza dough, prebake if needed for a crispy crust.
  1. Spread a generous layer of your favorite BBQ sauce on the base.
  1. Add cooked chicken, red onions, and green peppers.
  2. Sprinkle mozzarella or your preferred cheese evenly.
  3. Pop it in a hot oven until the crust is golden and cheese bubbly.
